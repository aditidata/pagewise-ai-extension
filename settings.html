How to Use
Get started in seconds
1️⃣
Choose your backend — Groq is easiest (just paste your free API key). Ollama is for privacy-first users.
2️⃣
Open any PDF in Chrome — local files or PDFs from the web.
3️⃣
Click the PageWise AI icon → Check Current Page → your PDF opens with AI summaries side by side.
💬
Chat with your PDF — switch to the Chat tab and ask anything about the current page.
